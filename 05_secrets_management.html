9 Secrets Management (ESM/Vault)

Private key custody, secrets storage, and credential rotation for the Cequence platform are managed by the Cequence SaaS vendor and the OKAPI platform team. This operating model does not own or operate HashiCorp Vault, AWS Secrets Manager, or any HSM/KMS infrastructure directly. The Enterprise Secrets Management (ESM) platform, based on HashiCorp Vault, is the organisation’s target-state secrets control plane; its phased rollout (AWS EKS, Azure, on-premises PUMA) is governed by the ESM programme. For this operating model, the relevant constraint is that DCERTS remains the issuer of record for all production leaf certificates, and private keys must not cross custody domains (Invariant I4). Where Vault is adopted by Cequence or OKAPI operations teams, it must enforce per-custody-zone isolation (separate namespaces, auth roles, and audit scopes) and must not become a second issuance authority unless PKI governance explicitly delegates that role.
